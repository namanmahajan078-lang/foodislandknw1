Our Story

A Passion for Pure
Vegetarian Cuisine

Welcome to Food Island, a culinary oasis founded by Shubham Agrawal in the heart of Khandwa. We believe that vegetarian food can be just as exciting and flavorful as any cuisine in the world.

From our smoky tandoori paneer tikka to crispy South Indian dosas, every dish is prepared with love, premium ingredients, and time-tested recipes that deliver an unforgettable dining experience.
